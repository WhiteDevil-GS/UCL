Rules and Regulations

  • Team should have 4 players.
  • The baton must be passed within a changeover box, which is usually 10 meters long and located 10 meters before and after each leg.
  • The player who drops the baton must recover it.
  • Players should remain in their lanes after changes.
  • A team will be disqualified for preventing another competitor from passing.
  • A team will be disqualified for interfering with another competitor.
  • Interbranch teams only allowed for 1st year students.
  • Team should consist of players from the same year students.

Cost of Participation

200 Rupees per team
