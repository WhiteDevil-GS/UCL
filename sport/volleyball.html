Rules and Regulations

  • Team should contain 6+2 players.
  • No rotations.
  • Server should toss the ball before service (if missed once, the team will be reinstructed) and service to be done by every player.
  • On the line, the ball will be considered as in.
  • One rally has 3 touches in a team, but one player has only one touch per rally.
  • Body touches are counted.
  • Interbranch teams are only allowed for 1st-year students.
  • Team should consist of players from the same year students.

Cost of Participation

600 Rupees per team
