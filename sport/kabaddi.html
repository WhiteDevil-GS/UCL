Rules and Regulations

  • A match duration is 20 minutes.
  • Each half of 10 minutes each.
  • Each team should consist of 10 players (7 playing and 3 substitute players).
  • Maximum of 5 substitutions for each team. Substitutions during time out and half time are not counted.
  • Players are revived in the same order they were out.
  • Revival is not applicable for bonus, technical, and lona points.
  • Pursuit is allowed.
  • Jersey pulling is not allowed. If performed, 1 point will be awarded to the opposition team and the raider is safe.
  • Violent tackles are not allowed (like tackling by holding the neck, using the elbow).
  • If a team performs two empty raids in a row, the next raid must be a "Do-or-Die raid".
