Dodge Ball

Game Rules and Registration

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team should have 7 players.
  • Circle-shaped courtyard with a borderline.
  • One round lasts 7 minutes.
  • Players from one team will be inside the court and should try to escape the throws from the opponent.
  • Players of another team, from outside the court (at the border), should hit the opponent players inside the court below knee level.
  • There will be 3 rounds. Whoever grabs the first 2 wins will be the winners. If each team wins 1 round each, then there will be a toss for the 3rd round.
  • If any misshit (above knee level) occurs, players will be reinstructed only once. If misshits continue more than 5 times, the team will be disqualified.

Cost of Participation

500 Rupees per team
