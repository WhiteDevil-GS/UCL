Rules and Regulations

  • After taking 3 steps, the javelin is thrown directly forward with an over-the-shoulder motion.
  • It must land point first and need not stick in the ground.
  • Throwing without pointing at the ground is considered a foul.
  • The thrower should not step on or beyond a line at the end of the runway.
  • Throughout the throwing process, the javelin must be kept in an overhand position, i.e., over the shoulder.
  • The thrower cannot turn back until the throw is completed.
