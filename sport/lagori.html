Lagori

Game Rules and Registration

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team should consist of 7 players and a stack that contains 7 stones or a Lagori set.
  • There will be 3 rounds; whoever grabs the first 2 wins will be the winners. If each team wins 1 round, then there will be a toss for the 3rd round.
  • One team should throw the ball to the stack and should rebuild it without getting hit by the opponent.
  • In the throwing team, 5 players have the chance to hit the stack (each player has only 3 chances).
  • Only 5 minutes are available to rebuild the stack after hitting it.
  • After rebuilding the stack, that player should encircle the Lagori set and must shout "LAGORI." If not, a point will be given to the opponent team.
  • The opponent team should not allow the throwing team to rebuild the stack and try to hit the throwing team within 5 minutes.
  • Circle-shaped court for the game.
  • Interbranch teams are only allowed for 1st-year students.
  • The team should consist of players from the same year.

Cost of Participation

400 Rupees per team
