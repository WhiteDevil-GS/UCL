Dog and the Bone Game

Game Rules and Registration

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team must consist of 4 players.
  • Each player in the team will be given a number between 1 to 4.
  • A small ball is placed in the center of the playing field.
  • The referee calls a number.
  • The player with that number (number called by the referee) from both teams runs towards the center to grab the ball.
  • Once a player has the ball, they must run back to their team without being tagged (caught) by an opposing player.
  • If a player successfully returns to their team with the ball, their team scores 20 points.
  • If a player is tagged (caught) by an opposing player while running back to their team, then the opposing player scores 10 points.
  • The team with the highest score at the end of 2 rounds wins.

Cost of Participation

300 Rupees per team
