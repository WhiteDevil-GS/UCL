Free Fire Tournament Rules

  • Registration: Only the first 24 teams can register on a first-come, first-served basis. Only the Team Leader should register the form once.
  • Team Composition: Team members must be from BIET Engineering College only. No team member exchange is allowed after registration.
  • Device Requirements: All maps (Bermuda, Kalahari, Purgatory) must be downloaded. iPads and emulators are not permitted. Devices must be fully charged; power banks are recommended.
  • Tournament Format:
    • There are 24 fixed teams for the tournament, divided into two groups (Group A and Group B).
    • Each group will play their first match on the Bermuda map.
    • From each group, the top 6 teams will qualify for the next round.
    • The top 6 teams from Group A and Group B will then play on the Purgatory map.
    • The top 2 teams from this match will qualify for the final.
    • The final will be a custom match on a Clash Squad map, and the winning team will be crowned the champions.
  • Arrival Time: All players must arrive at the venue at the designated time. Matches will start on time, and if any player is absent, it is the team's responsibility.
  • Team Size: Each team must have 4 members. If any player is absent, it is the team's responsibility, and the match will proceed as scheduled.
  • Scoring System: A volunteer will spectate and update the scores. Points will be awarded as follows:
    • 1 kill = 1 point
    • Position points:
      • 6th place = 1 point
      • 5th place = 3 points
      • 4th place = 4 points
      • 3rd place = 5 points
      • 2nd place = 6 points
      • Booyah (1st place) = 10 points
  • Screenshot Requirement: After each match, all players must take a screenshot of the match/lobby details after their team is eliminated. This is required for volunteer cross-checking in case of any discrepancies.
  • Room ID and Password: Custom Room ID and password will be provided at the venue. All players must join within 3-4 minutes.

Warnings

  • Venue Rule: All players must be present at the venue to play. Playing from outside the venue is strictly prohibited.
  • Time Rule: Players must arrive exactly on time at the venue, as matches will start promptly.
  • Suspicious Behavior: If any player is caught with suspicious behavior (such as cheating), the entire team will be banned immediately.
