Rules and Regulations

  • The shot putter must put the shot from a standing position within the circle.
  • The shot must be released with one hand and cannot be dropped.
  • The athlete must not step out of the circle during the throw.
  • The shot must land within the sector marked on the ground.
  • Each athlete will have three attempts to achieve their best throw.
  • The best throw will be recorded, and the athlete with the longest distance will win.
