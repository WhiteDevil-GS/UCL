Game Rules and Registration

Rules and Regulations

  • Team should contain 9 players.
  • Rotations are allowed.
  • Server should toss the ball before service (if missed, the team will be reinstructed) and service to be done by every player.
  • On the line, the ball will be considered as in.
  • Body touch and double touch are not allowed.
  • Two players can't touch the ball simultaneously.
  • The referee's decision is final.

Cost of Participation

600 Rupees per team
