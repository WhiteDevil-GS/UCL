Game Rules and Registration

Rules and Regulations

  • Toss shall be conducted before a game starts. If you win, you can choose between serving first or side of court you need.
  • The match consists of 3 sets. Each set is of 11 points. One who wins two sets is considered the winner. There will be changes of court after each set.
  • The server and receiver must be inside the service courts, and the serve must be diagonal to the opponent’s service court.
  • Serve from the right service court when the server’s score is even. Serve from the left service court when the server’s score is odd.
  • Shuttlecock on the line is considered as in.
  • Referee decision is final.
  • During serving, if the shuttlecock hits the net, the opponent will get the point.
  • If the shuttlecock hits the net and reaches the opponent's court, they can continue the rally. If it comes back to the same court, then the opponent gets the point.
