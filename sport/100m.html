100m Sprint

Game Rules and Registration

Rules and Regulations

  • All participants must be ready at the starting line before the race begins.
  • False starts will lead to immediate disqualification from that race.
  • The race will be held on a standard track, and participants must stay within their designated lanes.
  • The winner will be determined by the first participant to cross the finish line.
  • In case of a tie, a photo finish will be used to determine the winner.

Cost of Participation

70 Rupees per head
