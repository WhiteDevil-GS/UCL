BGMI Tournament Rules

  • Registration: Only the first 25 teams can register on a first-come, first-served basis. Only the Team Leader should register the form once.
  • Team Composition: Team members must be from BIET Engineering College only. No team member exchange is allowed after registration.
  • Device Requirements: Download all required maps: Payload, TDM, Arena, Quickmatch, Shanok, Miramar, Livik. iPads and emulators are not permitted. Devices must be fully charged; power banks are recommended.
  • Gameplay Rules: Join the game room within 5 minutes after receiving the Room ID and Password. Sharing of ID and Password is strictly prohibited; violators will be banned from future tournaments. Players must join their allotted slots only. Absence of a player leads to disqualification without a refund.
  • Conduct: Toxic behavior, harassment, or unsportsmanlike conduct will lead to disqualification. Server hacking will result in a permanent ban from the tournament.
  • Equipment: Gaming accessories like headphones and finger sleeves are allowed; external triggers are not permitted. Wi-Fi is not provided; players must use their own SIM network.

BGMI Tournament Format

  • Warm-up Match: PAYLOAD 3.0
  • 1st Match: Miramar - Top 20 teams will qualify for the next match.
  • 2nd Match: Shanok - Top 15 teams will move to the next round.
  • 3rd Match: Erangel - Top 7 teams will qualify for the semi-finals.
  • Semi-Final: Quick Match (Only Melee) - Top 2 teams will go to the final.
  • Final Match: TDM (Only M24) - The winner will be selected. Kills don't matter.
  • Chicken Dinner team will be considered as the top 1, then follows the next team as top 2, top 3, and so on.
